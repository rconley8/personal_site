About me

My name is Richard Conley and I am currently a Computer Science student at Otterbein University. I am passionate about classic hand-drawn animation, modern CGI, comics, web development and mobile development.

When I'm not engaged in one of the above, I enjoy attending many different conferences. My "regulars" are Ohio Linux Fest, PyOhio and Mid-Ohio Comic-Con.

I currently work for Network Technologies International, Inc. I am a Technical Support Analyst specializing in our DRNetOPEN product. DRNetOPEN extends DRNet's HP NonStop Server-based data replication product line to other hardware environments. DRNetOPEN collects database updates from NonStop Servers and uses our powerful messaging middleware to replicate data to most other kinds of hardware platforms, including Windows, Unix, and Linux.
